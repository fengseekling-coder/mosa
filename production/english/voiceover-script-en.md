# MOSA Demo English Voice-over

## Segment 1 · 00:00

This is a complete workflow from image generation to editing and archiving. We begin with a new task in Codex, then generate images directly on the Cowart canvas.

## Segment 2 · 00:22

Three different visual styles appear in sequence. Each image can be selected, moved, resized, and rearranged directly on the canvas.

## Segment 3 · 00:44

Once generation is complete, every asset is automatically added to MOSA. The gallery brings all results together, while preserving the prompt, model, dimensions, and source.

## Segment 4 · 01:10

Next, we return to Codex and create architectural, fashion, and material studies. Different formats and mediums all remain part of the same workflow.

## Segment 5 · 01:43

Each artwork can be opened on its own, reviewed in detail, refined further, or saved directly to the library.

## Segment 6 · 02:07

Finally, MOSA reconnects the work created across Codex and Cowart, while keeping every source and parameter traceable.

## Segment 7 · 02:26

From generation and editing to archiving and reuse, the entire creative pipeline is complete.

# 黑白极简概念 cover prompt

You are a top-tier cover art director, editorial visual designer, typography designer, and image-generation prompt director.

Create one single custom vertical poster cover image with aspect ratio 9:16.

The cover must use the selected visual style: 黑白极简概念 / black-white-minimal-concept. This style is not a decorative filter. Every major cover decision must be implemented through giant readable typography, restrained black-white-gray structure, precise grid alignment, negative space, light paper grain, and one accurate visual metaphor embedded into the title system.

- Title/topic: WOO 因台风巴威暂停营业通知
- Title hierarchy:
  - A-layer / main visual title: 暂停营业
  - B-layer / complete title: WOO 因 9 号台风“巴威”影响自 07 月 11 日起暂停营业
  - C-layer / subtitle or small text: 安全优先 / 恢复时间另行通知 / WOO 品牌艺术中心
- Platform: custom poster
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Language: 中文为主，少量英文机械标识可作为微型装饰
- Use case: WOO 粉丝玩家通知封面、社交媒体竖版公告海报、品牌艺术中心临时闭店视觉
- Short context summary: 受 9 号台风“巴威”影响，WOO 为保障玩家和工作人员安全，自 07 月 11 日起暂停营业，恢复时间另行通知。画面需要传达安全、秩序、临时封锁和风雨之后再相聚。
- Visual subject: 科幻机械舱门、锁定的红色安全闸、台风路径线、被停机的机械核心或控制终端，由巨型中文标题结构内部生长出来。

- Mood: 冷峻、理性、克制、未来、警戒、秩序、安全优先。
- Visual metaphor: 巨型“暂停营业”像一扇被机械锁定的安全舱门；细红线像台风路径和警戒封条，穿过字形但不遮挡可读性；黑色机械结构暗示系统临时停机保护。
- Banned elements: 不要出现真实灾难破坏场面、受伤人物、洪水惨状、廉价科幻游戏 UI、商业促销感、二维码、水印、Logo 乱贴、满屏正文、错别字、标题不可读、花哨多彩背景。

## Content Understanding

Understand the source announcement before composing the image, but do not output analysis in the image. Use only the derived fields above. Do not paste the full announcement body into the image, prompt, metadata, or small text system.

The cover must communicate at first glance that WOO is temporarily closed, and at second glance reveal the reason: Typhoon Bavi, safety-first temporary closure, and future reopening notice. The image should feel like a deliberate editorial poster and a refined sci-fi mechanical notice, not a generic information card.

## Cover Objective

Generate a finished 9:16 editorial poster. The main title “暂停营业” must be complete, accurate, and clearly readable. The complete meaning must be preserved through the B-layer line: “WOO 因 9 号台风‘巴威’影响自 07 月 11 日起暂停营业”. Use only a small amount of supporting text.

The poster should be suitable for WOO fans and visitors: serious, protective, branded, and visually memorable without becoming sensational.

## Style Application

Apply the selected style's non-negotiable anchors:

- Black, white, and gray editorial restraint.
- Giant readable title as the structural backbone.
- Sharp grid, large negative space, and precise alignment.
- One accurate visual metaphor embedded in typography.
- Light paper grain and minimal semantic accent color.
- Main title should become an architectural/mechanical structure: a wall, screen, container, safety door, or locked terminal.
- Subtitle and labels should be sparse modern editorial microtype.
- Visual subject should grow from the title structure rather than sit beside it.
- Preserve high title legibility, restrained composition, and only 1-3 clear metaphor anchors.

For this poster, the semantic accent color is red. Red is the theme color and must appear as a restrained warning system: thin safety rails, a locked status bar, a typhoon path line, small alert marks, and one concentrated red glow. Black is the auxiliary color and carries the mechanical body, typography mass, and negative-space pressure. White and gray provide the paper field and engineered silence.

Do not mention sci-fi or mechanical traits unless they are visible through clean industrial geometry, panel seams, bolts, rails, terminal indicators, mechanical shutters, or precise warning-line structures.

## Composition

Design a vertical 9:16 poster with a tall engineered grid.

- Primary visual center: the giant Chinese title “暂停营业” occupying the central vertical mass, built like a black mechanical safety door or locked terminal wall.
- Secondary visual elements: a thin red typhoon path line crossing the poster diagonally, one red lock/status indicator, sparse microtype labels such as “TYPHOON BAVI / SAFETY MODE / 07.11”, and a small footer line “WOO 品牌艺术中心”.
- Background or space system: mostly white or pale gray paper-grain field with black mechanical panel geometry, controlled margins, and precise grid ticks.
- Foreground/background layering: typography is the architecture; the mechanical shutter seams, warning rails, and red line are integrated into and around the strokes of the characters; no separate unrelated illustration.
- Reading path: first read “暂停营业”, then scan the red warning path and B-layer complete title, then the footer “恢复时间另行通知 / WOO 品牌艺术中心”.
- Shareability constraint: the topic must remain legible in a fast social feed, especially on mobile.

Use black, white, and gray as the dominant system. Use red as the only semantic accent and theme color. The red should be precise, mechanical, and warning-like rather than decorative: deep signal red, matte red ink, or small luminous red indicators.

Materials and textures:

- matte black mechanical panels and typographic slabs
- white or pale gray paper-grain background
- subtle halftone or scanline texture only where it supports the engineered poster feel
- thin red vector-like path line and warning marks

The result must feel like a finished high-end modernist sci-fi mechanical poster, not a colorful cyberpunk illustration.

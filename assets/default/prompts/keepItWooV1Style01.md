# 黑白极简概念 cover prompt

You are a top-tier cover art director, typography designer, and image-generation prompt director.

Create one single custom vertical cover for a physical 102 cm wide × 208 cm high panel. Preserve the exact portrait aspect ratio 51:104. Use exactly one selected visual style: 黑白极简概念 / black-white-minimal-concept. The style is the organizing language of the whole cover, not a filter.

## Input

- Content mode: editorial-cover
- Title/topic: KEEP IT WOO
- A-layer main title: KEEP IT WOO
- B-layer complete title: KEEP IT WOO
- Subtitle: none
- Language: English
- Platform/use: large-format entrance cover and spatial poster
- Aspect ratio: 51:104 portrait, based on 102 × 208 cm
- Visual subject: one perfectly circular futuristic holographic gate with clearly separated structural layers, opening symmetrically along a vertical center seam
- Mood: futuristic, ceremonial, precise, controlled, premium
- Visual metaphor: an encrypted circular threshold recognizes the viewer and begins to open—an “open sesame” moment
- Required verbatim display copy: KEEP IT WOO
- Audience: visitors entering an immersive space, exhibition, or premium technology environment
- Input Image 1 role: content and opening-gesture reference only; borrow the centered circular access mechanism and imminent doorway movement, not the person, hallway, photograph, or exact design
- Input Image 2 role: circular vector-construction reference only; borrow ring hierarchy, broken arcs, ticks, blocks, and readable spacing, not its exact artwork or colors

## Cover Objective

The first glance must identify the title and one circular opening mechanism. The second glance must reveal clear layers, a central split, and the selected style’s material or spatial logic. Keep all non-text visual structure left-right symmetrical; typography may break symmetry. Details must remain clearly distinguishable at large format.

## Style Application

Build one monumental circular topological gate from luminous threads, fine particles, contour paths, wireframe membranes, and data points inside a continuous black-to-silver tonal field. Keep museum-grade restraint, a sharp grid, and generous negative space.

## Composition

Place the circular gate slightly above center. Two mirrored topological halves pull apart around a narrow vertical void. Embed KEEP IT on the left opening membrane and WOO on the right membrane so the title becomes the gate bridge rather than a caption.

Keep the main circular mechanism front-facing and geometrically true, not an ellipse. Leave production-safe margins. Use one visual center, one metaphor, and no competing secondary scene.

## Image–Text Relationship

The exact phrase “KEEP IT WOO” must appear once and must be physically integrated into the selected style’s subject or material. It may become a structural band, wall, panel, cutout, membrane, block construction, architectural sign, printed strip, or connected identity module as specified above. Do not place it as an unrelated caption. Spell it exactly: K-E-E-P, space, I-T, space, W-O-O.

## Typography

Use a bold, clean sans-serif typeface, uppercase, with complete readable letterforms. Do not misspell, duplicate, mirror, crop, over-distort, or replace characters. Do not add any other readable slogan, logo, brand name, or filler copy.

## Color, Material, and Texture

Black, charcoal, silver gray, and white only, with at most one tiny cold activation point.

Every material, texture, line, shadow, and color decision must visibly belong to 黑白极简概念.

## Negative Constraints

Avoid generic flat HUD circles, colorful poster treatment, empty big-type cover, smoke, decorative geometry unrelated to the opening. Also avoid missing title, unreadable title, unrelated decoration, generic PPT or course-cover layout, e-commerce advertisement, watermark, contact sheet, grid of options, and multiple final designs.

## Final Standard

Generate only one finished image. It must be a complete 黑白极简概念 interpretation of the same KEEP IT WOO circular-opening brief, clearly distinct from all other styles while preserving the exact content, tall ratio, integrated title, and opening metaphor.

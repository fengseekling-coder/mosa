1. A full-height architectural boundary that establishes the 102 × 208 cm door/panel scale.
2. One dominant circular gate mechanism with multiple readable vector layers.

Use a deep carbon-black background, two graphite levels, pale silver hairlines, and warm off-white typography. Depth comes from vector opacity, overlapping planes, line density, and negative-space cuts. Keep light effects minimal and fully vector-based.
